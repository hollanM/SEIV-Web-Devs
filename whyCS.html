WHY BECOME A CS MAJOR?

Why CS? It turns curiosity into real projects—apps, AI tools, games, and solutions people actually use. You'll learn to break down hard problems, build with a team, and ship. Skills transfer everywhere, from startups to health, sports, and finance.

CS is structured creativity. You'll practice problem-solving, build portfolio projects, and learn tools used by real companies. The habits you build here—focus, iteration, collaboration—open doors in any field.

Major in CS to make ideas real. From web to AI, you'll learn core concepts, work in teams, and graduate with projects you can show. High demand, flexible careers, and the chance to make technology more fair, accessible, and useful.
